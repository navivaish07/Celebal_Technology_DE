## Key Findings

* The dataset contains around 1000 products from different categories.
  
* Some columns had missing values, especially seller and customer review-related information, which were handled during data cleaning.
  
* Price columns were cleaned and converted into numeric format so that calculations and analysis could be performed.
  
* Most products have ratings above 4, showing that customers are generally satisfied with the products.
  
* A few categories contain more products than others, making them the most active categories in the dataset.
  
* The popularity score helped identify products that have both good ratings and a large number of reviews
  
* A few products have unusually high prices compared to the rest of the dataset.


## Business Implications

* Products with high ratings and many reviews can be promoted more because customers already trust them.
  
* Popular categories can be given more attention in marketing campaigns and inventory planning.
  
* Customer ratings can help businesses understand which products are performing well and which need improvement.
  
* Different pricing ranges show that the platform serves both budget-conscious and premium customers.
  
* Products with low ratings should be reviewed to identify possible quality or service issues.
  
* Understanding customer preferences and product performance can help businesses make better decisions about pricing, promotions, and product selection.
